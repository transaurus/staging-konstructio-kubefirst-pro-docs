We want to hear from you! If you have questions about Kubefirst we'd love to help, [reach out to us on Slack](https://konstructio.slack.com).

Our `#helping-hands` channel is monitored by our team to help address questions and troubleshoot any issues you have.

- For many topics we recommend reviewing the docs here, or checking out the [earlier content for Kubefirst open source.](https://kubefirst.konstruct.io/docs/)
- For questions on pricing [view details here.](https://konstruct.io/kubefirst-pro#pricing)
