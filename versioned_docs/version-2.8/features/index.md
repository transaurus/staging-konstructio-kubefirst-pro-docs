## Summary

Kubefirst includes features and capabilities to manage your clusters, platform, and applications. These capabilities are available regardless of your cloud.

Kubefirst Pro or Kubefirst UI features are available to all users in the Free tier and include your management cluster. Upgrade to Business supports access to the Kubefirst UI for up to 10 workload clusters in a single cloud.

After your installation, explore more about using Kubefirst here. For information on topics like upgrading, managing users, and deprovisioning check out [Kubefirst Administration](../admin/).
